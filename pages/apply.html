Opportunities for PhD, MS students, postdocs, and visiting researchers

Open Positions

PhD Students

Available

We are looking for highly motivated PhD students interested in computer vision, machine learning, and AI research. Candidates should have strong mathematical background and programming skills.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
  • Strong programming skills in Python, PyTorch/TensorFlow
  • Solid mathematical foundation in linear algebra, statistics, and optimization
  • Experience with machine learning and computer vision (preferred)
  • Strong English communication skills

What we offer:

  • Competitive stipend and research funding
  • State-of-the-art computing resources and equipment
  • Opportunities to publish at top-tier conferences
  • International collaboration and conference travel support
  • Mentorship from experienced researchers

Master's Students

Available

MS positions are available for students interested in gaining research experience in computer vision and machine learning.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • Programming experience (Python preferred)
  • Strong motivation for research
  • Good academic record

Postdoctoral Researchers

Limited

Postdoc positions for outstanding researchers with expertise in our research areas. Candidates should have a proven track record of high-quality publications.

Requirements:

  • PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or related field
  • Strong publication record in top-tier venues
  • Independent research experience
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Visiting Researchers

Available

We welcome visiting researchers, including international students and faculty, for short-term or long-term research collaboration.

Requirements:

  • Current enrollment in PhD program or faculty position
  • Research interests aligned with our lab
  • Recommendation from current advisor/institution
  • Self-funded or external funding support

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

We accept applications year-round, but recommend applying by March 1st for Fall admission and September 1st for Spring admission.

Do I need prior research experience?

While research experience is preferred, it's not strictly required. We value strong motivation, technical skills, and willingness to learn.

Is financial support available?

Yes, we provide competitive stipends and research funding for PhD and MS students, as well as conference travel support.

Can international students apply?

Absolutely! We welcome applications from international students and provide support for visa applications and settling in Korea.
