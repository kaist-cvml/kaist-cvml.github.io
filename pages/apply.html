Frequently Asked Questions

How often are applications reviewed?

Applications are reviewed every three months unless otherwise notified. We will contact selected candidates for interviews during these review periods.

Do I need to complete the Google Form?

Yes, completing our online application form is mandatory for all applicants. You cannot be considered without submitting this form first.

Should I apply for internship first?

We highly recommend applying to our lab's internship program or KAIST AI Research Internship (KAIRI) before formally joining our lab. This helps both parties assess fit.

What's the difference between lab application and KAIST admission?

Our lab application is for research position selection, while KAIST Graduate School admission is the official enrollment process. Both are required but separate.

Do I need prior research experience?

While research experience is preferred, it's not strictly required. We value strong motivation, technical skills, and willingness to learn.

Is financial support available?

Yes, we provide competitive stipends and research funding for PhD students, as well as conference travel support.

Can international students apply?

Absolutely! We welcome applications from international students and provide support for visa applications and settling in Korea.
